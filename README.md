> **Your team is paying for Slack twice. This agent finds it in 30 seconds.**

Most companies bleed 15–30% of their SaaS budget on duplicate or unused tools. Manual audits take days and get skipped. The **Subscription Optimizer Agent** reads your expense CSV, runs a mathematical duplicate-detection script, and generates a cut-ready SOP — before your next finance meeting.

---

### 💎 Enterprise Features

* **🔢 Mathematical Duplicate Detection:** Uses `analyze_csv.py` to identify identical tool names programmatically — no human interpretation bias.
* **📊 Redundancy Matrix:** A structured table of every duplicate tool, how many seats are paid, and the estimated annual waste.
* **🛠️ Actionable 3-Step SOP:** Outputs a Standard Operating Procedure to cancel, merge, or renegotiate each identified contract.
* **⚡ No Dependencies:** Works with a plain CSV export from any expense tool (QuickBooks, Ramp, Brex, etc.).
